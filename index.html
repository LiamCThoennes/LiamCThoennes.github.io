Everything is Political

The Moral Imperative of Public Speech in a Democracy

This is for every influencer, every celebrity, every athlete who has ever said, "I'm not political."

This paper is an argument against that excuse. It is the case for why, in a democracy teetering on the brink, that statement is not just a falsehood; it is a cowardly and complicit act.
